OUR HISTORY

Our Journey at Allied Global (AG) started more than ten years back in United Arab Emirates with a vision to create a business that would provide reliable and high-quality energy products to customers around the world. In its early years, the company focused on building a strong reputation for quality, reliability, and customer service.

Through hard work and dedication, the company established itself as a reliable and reputable brand in the industry, attracting a loyal customer base that valued our commitment to excellence. Initially the focus remained on lubricants and petrochemicals. However, over the period of time, sourcing of other products kept on adding to our inventory and today we take pride in saying that we are a reliable supplier for full spectrum of petroleum and energy requirements around the globe.

As AG grew, it expanded its operations to new regions and markets, establishing partnerships with leading oil and gas producers and suppliers all over the world. These partnerships allowed the company to secure a steady supply of high-quality energy products, while also ensuring that it remained competitive in an increasingly crowded market. The journey included venturing into production, both upstream and downstream supply chains. However the main focus always remained on O&G trading and we take special pride in having our name established as a globally reputable and reliable brand.

Over time, the company invested heavily in technology and infrastructure, building a state-of-the-art trading platform that allowed it to operate efficiently and effectively in global markets. This investment in technology, combined with a deep understanding of market trends and customer needs, enabled the company to adapt quickly to changing market conditions and stay ahead of the competition.

Today, AG under the patronage of our esteemed Chairman is a leading player in the global oil and gas trading industry, with a reputation for excellence and a track record of success. It has established itself as a trusted partner to clients around the world, delivering reliable and high-quality petroleum and energy products that meet their specific needs. And with a strong commitment to sustainability and corporate social responsibility, the company is well positioned to continue its growth and success for many years to come.

ALLIED GLOBAL

Allied Global is a leading petroleum and energy sourcing & trading company operating in UAE since 2011 specializing in supply of full spectrum of Oil & Gas products and derivatives, coal and asphalt. Its track record displays a depth of experience and expertise that spans across sectors and regions. It specifically looks after the full spectrum of energy and petroleum requirements with clientele spread all over the world. Allied Global also carries a varied portfolio of energy investments with focus on promoting green energy.
